# Summary

Tired of screwdrivers rolling around every time you open or close your tool drawer? I was.
Here's a simple solution to keep screwdrivers in place and maximize drawer space. Print as many as you need, I've included all the files so you can modify if needed.

Measures 225mm x 40mm x 23mm high. Prints at a 45 degree angle to fit on a 200mm x 200mm bed with room for skirt and brims if needed.

Holds 5 screwdrivers from one direction and 4 screwdrivers from the other. Holds all common small, medium and large sized screwdrivers to a max shaft diameter of 10mm (won't hold those tiny narrow handled precision screwdrivers very well).
