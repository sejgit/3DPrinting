# Summary

My family ate all of the Leaf Cookies I made for my last cookie cutter installment, so I decided to make this Thanksgiving Cookie Cutter Set for the upcoming holiday.  Chestnuts, Cornucopia, Oak Leaves, Pumpkin, and Turkey.

I can't decide yet whether to make Sugar Cookies, Gingerbread Cookies, or Peanut Butter Cookies.  Maybe all three.

I decided to make Gingerbread!!!

I am including my standard Sugar Cookie Recipe with these files.
