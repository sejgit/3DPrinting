# Summary

It annoys me every time to remove all roles to exchange only one. Below is enough space to stack silicate bags. Thanks to the ball bearing and Teflon tube, there is less friction than before. (with PVC pipe) I fixed the holders with hot glue in the corners. You just have to drill the 5 holes for Teflon tubes (6mm).
