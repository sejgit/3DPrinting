# Summary

Original bearing holders from chertle 
https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2873244
had a to large gap between bearing and holder. Some of filament rolls I use have small edges and fall into the gap. They also turned arround on the rod which i don't like. 

This version has almost no gap and don't turn arround because they lean on the samla box walls.
